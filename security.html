Security and Compliance

Designed for healthcare trust from the start.

Medivo’s MVP delivery plan treats legal, privacy, security, and compliance readiness as launch-blocking work, not back-office paperwork. The platform is intended to support privacy impact work, access control, audit logging, breach readiness, and secure operations before pilot launch.

Alberta-first privacy posture

Built with Alberta health privacy expectations in mind, including privacy impact assessment planning and access governance.

Role-based access

Clinicians, administrators, billing users, support teams, and patients receive role-appropriate access only.

Audit readiness

Core user activity, patient chart access, edits, exports, authentication events, and AI draft provenance are logged.

Data residency planning

Designed around Canadian hosting and deployment controls for sensitive healthcare workflows.

AI controls

AI scribe review remains clinician-approved, traceable, and not autonomous diagnostic or treatment software in the MVP.

Security baseline

MFA, encryption, CI/CD, secure coding practices, monitoring, backup and restore controls are part of the MVP path.

Incident readiness

Breach response procedures, escalation paths, and stakeholder responsibilities are defined before launch.

Legal gates

PIA planning, privacy policy, terms of use, data terms, and go-live approval are included as critical milestones.
