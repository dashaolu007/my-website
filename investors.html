Investor Overview

A focused EMR MVP with a broader digital health platform path.

Medivo is positioned to start with family physician clinics in Alberta, prove adoption through a paid pilot launch, and expand into additional Canadian provinces with premium modules and interoperability over time.

Investment thesis

Medivo is not being built as a generic EMR. It is a workflow and automation platform for primary care with long-term potential in AI documentation, forms automation, virtual care, billing intelligence, and connected health infrastructure.
