# Medivo Website Package - TSC-Inspired Format

This package rebuilds the Medivo website as a general health technology company website, using the referenced TSC-style structure:
- Home
- Products & Services
- Projects
- Partners
- News
- About
- Contact
- Top CTA
- Hero
- About section
- Core Expertise
- Service Excellence
- Testimonials
- Client/Partner ecosystem
- Newsletter/contact CTA
- Footer

## Medivo Positioning

Medivo is positioned as a general health technology company. Medivo EMR is the star player, but the site also includes:
- Physician Portal
- Patient Portal
- AI Scribe & Clinical Assistant
- Forms Automation
- Virtual Care & Secure Messaging
- Billing & Revenue Cycle
- Analytics & Population Insights
- Medivo Connect Interoperability Hub
- Managed HealthTech Services
- Health Data Security & Compliance
- Pharmacy & Lab Connectivity

## Inquiry Routing
